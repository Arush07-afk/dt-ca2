Emergency Solution - Heat Stroke

1

Call Emergency Services Immediately

This is a critical medical emergency, and delaying professional medical attention can be fatal.

2

Move the person to a cooler place

Get them into an air-conditioned environment, shade, or any area away from direct sun exposure.

3

Remove excess clothing

Loosen or take off unnecessary layers of clothing to help the body cool down more effectively

4

Begin rapid cooling immediately

Use whatever means available to lower their body temperature quickly:

Immerse the person in a cool bath or shower.

Apply ice packs or cool, wet towels to the neck, armpits, and groin.

Spray the person with cool water and fan them to promote evaporative cooling.

Disclaimer: These steps are solely for educational purposes, in case of any emergency, contact the helpline and always seek professional medical help. Do not rely solely on this guide for life-threatening emergencies.
